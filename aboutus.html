About Our Organisation

Mother of mercy support and care organisation is a non-governmental organization started in Chimba village in Mungwi district of Zambia in the year 2019 by Mr. Friday M. Mulenga and his family and was later introduced to Bwebe village in Kasama district of Northern Province, Zambia.
Mr. Mulenga and his family have a passion for the vulnerable in society was using his own income to support the widows, orphans, and street kids by helping them through difficult situations and become self-reliant through the provision of trade skills and material assistance.

It is this background that has inspired many to join Mr. Friday M. Mulenga for his noble call on the vulnerable and to ensure that the mother of mercy support and care organization is formerly registered before the end of the year 2022

A Governing Committee consisting of 11 people elected democratically by its committee. MOMSCO runs on a fully decentralized management system headed by the country coordinator, who is supported by a group of professional and administrative staff. Program and performances are monitored on a regular basis in quarterly review and planning meetings.
Annual General Meeting (AGM) is held annualy in the first week of December each year at a time and place to be fixed by the executive committee.Eminent experts from the field of management, agriculture, environment, and social work, etc. are invited to these meetings.

A special General meeting of the organization shall be held:- (1) On the request of the executive committee (2) On the request of 2/3 members of the Organization delivered in wailing to the Secretary.

Organisational structure

MOMSCO believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture and environment. MOMSCO puts a lot of emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff. For this purpose, need-based training is regularly organized

Vision

We aim at ending poverty together and create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is to be a leading organization in socio-economic, cultural development of poor people and vulnerable groups in the province and beyond. Biblical principles is at the core business of our day-to-day operations. Showing wisdom, integrity, faithfulness and accountability.
