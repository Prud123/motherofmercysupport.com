CORONA VIRUS

As much as COVID-19 is a global health crisis, it is also a human crisis. Prevention such as vaccines is one of the most effective measures to mitigate such a catastrophic public health crisis. Before the availability of the vaccine, precautions such as wearing a mask can potentially reduce the virus transmission rate. Recent modelling studies suggest that timely and comprehensive precautions and care are needed to prevent a second wave of COVID-19.In a free market, the risks of not having enough masks for the general population may negatively impact the outbreak. Also, it is the time of the festive season, like church gathering, Wedding season, and New-Year celebration, soon. After the removal of lockdown by the GOZ, people are taking the situation lightly. So it is a good time for making people aware that the deadliest virus is still there. The timely implementations of the public awareness program, free distribution of masks, and government advocacy and policies will decrease the COVID-19 spread as much as possible.

Action

The GOZ and WHO highlighted the importance of wearing the mask, and social distancing also told various dos’ and don’ts to defeat COVID-19. MOMSCO will develop a good collection of awareness materials in English, Bemba and other local languages, depending upon the target audience. MOMSCO will target on raising awareness about symptoms and prevention, and encouraging social distancing. The awareness materials will include printed posters and stickers for display in hospitals and public places and Questios and An on COVID-19, weekly community meetings, and radio announcements.

Impact

Due to the awareness program, people will be more conscious and will take precautions. Consequently, the spread of coronavirus will be decreased. In addition to this, the negative impact of the COVID-19 Outbreak on the socio-economic condition of the country will be minimized.
